SmoothOperator

Your semi-autonomous luggage assistant for accessible hospitality.

Problem & Vision

61 million Americans live with disabilities, many facing mobility challenges in airports. [CDC, 2020]

The post-pandemic travel boom and a 31% drop in hospitality labor force [BLS, 2022] have created a service bottleneck, especially for passengers needing assistance.

Our Vision

To restore independence and dignity to every traveler through intelligent, autonomous support.

By blending cutting-edge robotics with inclusive design, SmoothOperator makes airports more accessible, efficient, and human-centered.

System Design

Architecture Overview

Subsystems Overview

  • Mechanical: Lift platform, chassis, 10:1 gear train.
  • Electrical: Dual 12 V batteries, motor drivers, safety fuses.
  • Software: ROS navigation stack, touchscreen UI, mobile app.

Implementation & Results

We achieved reliable SLAM navigation, dynamic obstacle avoidance, and a secure mobile control interface. The system transports up to 80 lbs at 1.2 m/s with multiple safety redundancies.
