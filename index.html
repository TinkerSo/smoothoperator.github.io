SmoothOperator

Your semi-autonomous luggage assistant for accessible hospitality.

Problem & Vision

70% of airports face workforce shortages post-COVID

Labor shortages have left critical services like luggage handling under-supported in major airports. This impacts all travelers, but especially those with limited mobility or physical impairments.

1 in 4 U.S. adults has a disability

Whether it's navigating crowded terminals or managing heavy baggage, millions are left behind by current systems. The travel experience becomes exhausting, undignified, and unsafe for many.

SmoothOperator envisions a future where everyone travels with confidence.

Our semi-autonomous robotic assistant bridges the gap between technology and accessibility. By offering hands-free luggage handling and intelligent navigation, SmoothOperator empowers travelers of all backgrounds to move independently and with dignity.

System Design

Architecture Overview

Subsystems Overview

  • Mechanical: Lift platform, chassis, 10:1 gear train.
  • Electrical: Dual 12 V batteries, motor drivers, safety fuses.
  • Software: ROS navigation stack, touchscreen UI, mobile app.

Implementation & Results

We achieved reliable SLAM navigation, dynamic obstacle avoidance, and a secure mobile control interface. The system transports up to 80 lbs at 1.2 m/s with multiple safety redundancies.
